Finn walked past the shared shower rooms at the base and was stopped dead in his tracks when he heard a voice lilt out. "If I could try to be half of what you mean to me" The beautiful female voice was melded with a deeper melody of a males "I could be about anything, I could even learn how to love like you" Finn knew the giggles that followed the short tune. He knew those laughs so well. His loves could sing perfectly together. And Finn made a resolution to stop by the shower room more.
