Rey's first instinct when the girl with three names starts to follow her around so closely, talk at her so often, explain every little corner of the base, is to grip her staff a little tighter and walk faster. To bristle. She might not know what a duty roster is, but she isn't some gullible child. But Connix leaves her koyo melons every evening. And Kaydel Ko sits beside her during meditation and doesn't speak, just folds her legs and joins Rey in silence, for once. When Delly lays her hand on Rey's cheek and kisses her, Rey leans closer.
