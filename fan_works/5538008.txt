“So, I hear you’re a big deal,” Poe says, all flashing teeth and crinkling eyes. Finn shakes his head, his cheeks warming. “I don’t know where you heard that, but—” “What? It’s a lie? Maybe I should take this back, then.” Poe taps the jacket, fingers lingering on the collar. “You said it suits me,” Finn manages, very aware of the hand inches from his face. “It does.” Poe’s more serious now, but his eyes still spark. “But I only let important people wear it.” “Well.” Finn can barely breathe from their closeness. “Maybe I am a big deal, then.”
